Research

Networking and distributed-systems researchers expend significant effort to answer some fundamental questions: How do we compute data efficiently at large scale? How do we ensure systems or protocols are reliable? How do we automatically and effectively manage resources?

My current and past research addresses questions relating to scalable, reliable, and manageable distributed systems. I am improving the performance of paralleled distributed transactions within the cloud-based data storage framework. Previously, I was rethinking network testbeds to provide more reliable and reproducible experiments in the evaluation of distributed applications and network protocols at scale. I was further building a distributed platform-as-a-service for automatically managing the implementation and deployment of information systems and business logic.
